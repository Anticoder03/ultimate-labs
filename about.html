About Ultimate Labs

Excellence in Medical Diagnostics Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ultimate Labs has been at the forefront of medical diagnostics in India. What started as a small laboratory has grown into one of the most trusted diagnostic centers in the country, serving thousands of patients with accuracy and care.

Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a preferred choice for healthcare providers and patients alike. We continuously invest in cutting-edge technology and training to ensure the highest standards of diagnostic services.

Today, we are proud to be NABL accredited and recognized for our contribution to healthcare through accurate and reliable diagnostic services.
